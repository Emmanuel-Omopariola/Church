Who We Are

Purpose & Mission

The purpose of The Quickening Assembly Ministries A.K.A Valiant Army Ministries is to honor and glorify God by fulfilling the Great Commission of Jesus Christ, equipping believers for service, and demonstrating the love of Christ through reconciliation, discipleship, and outreach.

Our Purpose in Action

  • Preach the Gospel: proclaim salvation and hope through Jesus Christ to all people (Mark 16:15).
  • Make Disciples: teach the Word, baptize believers, and raise mature followers of Christ (Matthew 28:19-20).
  • Build Believing Community: cultivate fellowship, prayer, worship, and mutual encouragement (Hebrews 10:24-25).
  • Serve with Compassion: show Christ's love through practical care for those in need (Matthew 25:35-40).
  • Equip Believers for Ministry: train members to serve and lead in church and daily life (Ephesians 4:12).
  • Reach Locally and Globally: witness in our community and beyond through outreach and missions (Acts 1:8).
  • Promote Holistic Growth: encourage spiritual, emotional, and social well-being in Christ (3 John 1:2).

Mission Statement

Our mission is to glorify God by making disciples, serving the community, and empowering believers to live out their faith. We fulfill this mission through Gospel-centered preaching, sound doctrine, practical service opportunities, and faithful outreach.

"And whatever you do, in word or deed, do everything in the name of the Lord Jesus..." — Colossians 3:17

Core Values

Faithfulness to Scripture

We submit to the authority of God's Word in teaching and daily living (2 Tim. 3:16-17).

Love and Compassion

We love as Christ loved and care for people with grace and truth (John 13:34-35).

Integrity and Transparency

We pursue honesty, accountability, and trust in every ministry decision (Prov. 10:9).

Servant Leadership

We lead by serving, following the example of Jesus Christ (Mark 10:45).

Accountability & Discipleship

We walk together in growth, correction, and restoration through love (Gal. 6:1-2).

Unity in Christ

We seek unity across backgrounds as one body advancing God's mission (Eph. 4:3).
